Our Services

Structural Design

Engineers of Infraup have experience of designing various type of R.C.C Structures, Steel Structures, Industrial Structures, Large Span Trusses, Retaining Structures, OHSR, UGSR using guidelines laid by latest Indian design codes.

Project Management

Engineers of Infraup have numerous completed projects with planning, resource and project management which includes construction in mountain terrains and high seismic zones. Also projects are tailored according to the budget, IS Codes, time and preferences set by the client.

Estimation & Costing

We have expertise in Estimation and costing as per various Schedules, Tender Documentation, Preparation of BOQ (Bill of Quantities), Steel Bar bending schedules.

Site Inspection Visits

We offer site inspection visits to ensure the quality of work. We also conduct several onsite tests and ensure that all the work is done as per the structural design.

About Us

InfraUp is a Civil and Structural Engineering consultancy with Engineers having working experience of more than 200 projects. Our engineers have vast experience in field ofStructural Design, Project Management and Construction. Our engineers are registered with Institution of Engineers (India)- Kolkata and HP TCP. We ensure quality work within given time.
